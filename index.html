About SignSpeak AI

SignSpeak AI leverages Machine Learning, OpenCV, and Text-to-Speech technology to translate sign language gestures into spoken words in real-time, enhancing communication for individuals with hearing and speech impairments.

Features

Gesture Recognition

Detects and interprets sign language gestures with precision using computer vision.

AI Translation

Uses machine learning to convert gestures into text.

Text-to-Speech

Transforms translated text into speech.
