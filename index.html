How SignSpeak AI Works

1

Hand Gesture Capture

Using OpenCV & MediaPipe to accurately detect and track hand movements in real-time.

2

AI Recognition

TensorFlow/PyTorch models classify gestures with an average accuracy of x% on ASL benchmarks.

3

Text-to-Speech Conversion

gTTS/Pyttsx3 then converts recognized signs to audible speech.

4

Real-Time Speech Output

Instant voice output enables conversations with minimal latency.
